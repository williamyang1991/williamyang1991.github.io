Hi, I'm Shuai Yang (杨 帅)

This is my personal website. I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology of Peking University (WICT). I'm a member of the Spatial and Temporal Restoration, Understanding and Compression Team (STRUCT) at WICT.
